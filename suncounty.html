        • Welcome to Sun County Roleplay!

          Sun County Roleplay is a FiveM community founded in November of 2023 with the goal of providing an engaging, fun, safe, and unique environment for our members. We utilize FiveM servers, upholding custom-designed assets and scripts which allow for a unique experience with custom assets. We are proud to be a community that values acceptance and respect for all individuals who wish to be a part of our community.

          Want to Join Sun County Roleplay?

          Sun County Roleplay is always open to welcome new individuals into our community. If you are interested in joining our immersive roleplay, please create an account on our website and provide the requested information. At this time, there is no required application, meaning you can freely join our discord and become a member of our community. Please ensure that you are following all posted regulations in order to verify. Do not DM anyone regarding your verification process or your verification will be voided, and you may lose the ability to join the Sun County Roleplay community.

          Community Age Requirement

          Sun County Roleplay is determined to ensure that our members will have the ability to experience a mature, respectful, and entertaining environment. To ensure that our members can abide by these expectations, our community age limit has been set to 16 years or older.

          SCRP Administration has the right to remove any member from the community if they do not uphold our community standards.
          Lying, and falsifying information provided to the SCRP Administration Team will result in permanent removal from the community.

          Thank You, Sun County Roleplay Administration
